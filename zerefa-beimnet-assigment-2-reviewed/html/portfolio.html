    Project 01: Graphic Design

    Concept: This project is an exploration of New York through digital illustration.

    Objective: The goal was to challenge myself by using Adobe Illustrator skills to design vector art in a distinct visual style of my own.

    Result: The final illustrations are scenes of the city captured through a vibrant and geometric vector art form.

    Project 02: Curation

    Concept: Lost and Found explores multiculturalism, recognizing the significance of shared experiences among individuals from different cultural backgrounds.

    Objective: The mission was to establish a space that celebrates and unites people of color from multiple cultural backgrounds residing in New York City; challenging the traditionally exclusive gallery experience.

    Result: The final show is a multimedia exhibition that delves into the themes of belonging, comfort, and human connection through visual storytelling. Its curation is in the form of photography, painting and sound.

    Project 03: Photography & Textile

    Concept: This piece is comprised of photographs from my recent trip in Ethiopia, all of which are taken in my childhood house.

    Objective: I wanted to recall the images of home, reimagining my childhood through the language of textile and embroidery.

    Result: The final piece is a photographic transfer print on organza fabric adorned with embroidery that extends each image beyond its borders.
